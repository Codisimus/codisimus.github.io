Hi, my name is Cody M. Sommer. I am 24 years old and live near Rochester, NY with my wife Ella and my daughter Hazel. I have earned my bachelors degree in Computer Science at SUNY Brockport and I currently work for Thomson Reuters.

In my spare time I enjoy programming, making videos/music, and playing games. Online, I go by the alias Codisimus and I am most known for my Minecraft server plugins. I have written several Bukkit plugins including ButtonWarp and PhatLoots. I have also done private work for some large public servers. I love programming for Minecraft and I have even written a book instructing others how to do the same. Sadly, I do not do private work or plugin requests anymore. However, if you are willing to buy my book and try to create a plugin yourself I will try to answer any questions that you have about Bukkit or developing with it.
