Fill out your bingo board by clicking on each square to select an option. The options describe different events, most of which will happen sometime throughout the game. The options include events on the field, in the stands, on the sidelines, and during the commercials. There are 35 options and only 25 spaces on the bingo board so chose your events wisely. Keep in mind that some spaces, such as the center space, can earn bingo in several directions. This may affect where you wish to place specific tiles.

There is a Fill Board button which will automatically fills the remaining squares on your board. One of the options reads Ad for {Insert brand} and requires a brand to filled in the field above the board. (This is intended to be a brand such as Bud Light, Hyundai, or Doritos) Once your board is completely filled, use the Generate Board button to save your board and prepare for play. The reset button at the bottom will clear all options on the board if you wish to choose all new options.

Once your board is generated, you can bookmark the page to visit it later or share it with others. When playing with others, if you are worried about cheating, take note of the generated ID at the bottom of each persons board.

As the events occur, mark them on your board by clicking on the square. Be sure to point out events as they happen so that there are others who witness them. As with normal bingo, the first person with 5 in a row wins! When someone wins, everyone must clear off their board and a new round can be started. You can clear the board by refreshing the webpage. You can rearrange or replace tiles between rounds if you wish.
